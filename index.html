News

12/08/15 - Post Festival Blues / New Beginnings

We all had an amazing time at Bloodstock Festival, we cant thank the people who came and watched us enough. We cannot wait to go back next year, it truly was the best festival we've been to.


We are also delighted to announce that we now are working closely with Krossfire Promotions - Watch this space for more announcements! Also keep a look out for our debut album, which we are releasing towards the end of the year on this website. Digital and Physical distribution will be available

21/07/15 - Bloodstock Festival 2015 / Mini-Tour

It's an honour to be officially announced for Bloodstock Festival 2015. We will be tearing apart the New Blood Stage on the Saturday and we hope we can see you all there!

We have a couple of surprises to give out around the festival so please come say Hi! We couldn't have got here without the backing of our Fans, Promoters and M2TM Liverpool (Shout out to Doc - Facemelting Entertainment)

We would also love to announce our Mini-Tour 2015 around August/Sept and onwards! If you can make a local show please come and support us, the other bands and the venue! Keep our local music scene alive.

27/06/15 - The Road to Bloodstock Festival - Final

Tonight we won Bloodstock's M2TM Liverpool Final and it couldn't have been any harder for Simon Hall to judge. All the bands deserved to be there / win it and they all seriously brought it!

We have to say a HUGE thankyou to everyone who has supported us and give us well wishes, we wouldn't be in the position without you. More information and news coming soon :) Thankyou!

25/06/15 - The Road to Bloodstock Festival (Continued)

We played Metal 2 The Masses - Semi-Final on Saturday and won outright. The band are still coming to terms with the win and we're now officially in the Finals!

We really do appreciate everyone voting and the judges for backing us this time! It's one of our dreams to play Bloodstock Festival. See you this Saturday for the final!!

08/06/15 - No Solitude (Official Music Video)

We'd like to announce that our new music video is finally out. This is a single from our upcoming debut album. Thanks to our guitarist Matt Pike for all the work he has put into this project. We hope you all enjoy!
